Training And Workshop

Training and workshop services are designed to enhance professional competencies through expert-led courses, personalized learning paths, and interactive workshops that provide practical, hands-on experience.

We provide specialized skilling and training services designed to enhance professional competencies through expert-led courses and personalized learning paths

  • Women Empowerment: We promote women empowerment through education, training, and advocacy.
  • Computing to coding in vernacular language: We provide training in computing and coding in vernacular languages to rural India, helping people to access digital opportunities and participate in the digital era.
  • Cyber Security: We provide education and training on cyber security to help people protect themselves from cyber threats.
  • Financial Literacy: We provide financial literacy training to help people make informed financial decisions and improve their financial well-being.
  • Mental Health: We promote mental health and well-being through education and awareness campaigns. We also provide support services to people with mental health conditions.
  • Khelo India football - Sports: We promote sports participation and development in India, with a focus on football. Our program aims to provide opportunities for young people to participate in sports and to develop their athletic skills.
  • Digital Kisan: We provide digital literacy training to farmers, helping them to access information and resources online. We also help farmers to develop and use digital tools to improve their productivity and income.
  • Soft Skills: We provide training in soft skills, such as communication, teamwork, and problem-solving & leadership. These skills are essential for success in the workplace and in life.
